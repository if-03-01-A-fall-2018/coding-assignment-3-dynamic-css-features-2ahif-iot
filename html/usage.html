Use cases of the IoT in important industry sectors

The Internet of Things has serious impact on many industry sectors.

Three sectors show the way:

Producing companies
The industrial IoT(IIot) changes the production totally.
Manufacturer use the IIoT and the M2M(machine to machine)-communication for the promotion of industrial automation, to predict and avoid machine failures. It's also used to increase the work safety.
Haulage firms
There are many optimization areas, because of the transporting with IoT-sensor: From the engine performace and safety to the management of logistics.
Automative industries
The IoT and machine learning are not only the requirement for self-driving cars.
They help to
  • prevent accidents.
  • prognose maintenance problems.
  • find a parking slot.

IoT at home

The Internet of Things is not only used in the insutrial world. It can make our daily life a lot more comfortable.
Here are some examples what you can use the IoT at home for:

  • Entertainment system e.g.: tv, gaming system, speaker and headphones
  • Heating and cooling systems, ceiling fan, carbon monoxide detector, smoke alarm, lights
  • Smart home appliances e.g.: refrigerator, coffee maker, oven, vacuum
  • Home security e.g.: alarms, smart locks, garage door openers, baby monitors, cameras, home assistants
