General information

Connected devices

IoT basically describes all (internet-)connected devices.
It also means that the devices are connected with other devices.

These consist of:

  • Sensors → They send or show data
    • Temperature sensors
    • GPS and magnetic field sensors
    • Light sensors
  • Actuators → Execute received operations
    • Switches
    • Buttons
    • Shutter actuators
    • Light bulbs
    • Door locks
    • Watering systems
  • Smart devices
    • Smartphones
    • Printers
    • Connected heating systems
    • Photovoltaic systems
    • Connected refrigerators
    • Stationary home assistents (Alexa, Google Home)

Examples

The Internet of Things made our lifes easier - and everyone is affected by it.

Due to the IoT we're able to track shipments over the web and get the position of a delivery service driver in real time.
Printers are able to show the ink levels - some of them are even able to order it from an online shop if needed.
